The Process

How $mart Cart works

From building your list to checking out smarter — here's exactly how $mart Cart saves you money and keeps you on track nutritionally.

Step by Step

Simple to use, powerful under the hood

No learning curve. Just smarter shopping in four steps.

1
Setup

Create your profile

Tell $mart Cart about yourself — your location, dietary goals, food preferences, and your weekly grocery budget. This takes less than two minutes and unlocks personalized recommendations from day one.

  • Set calorie and macro targets
  • Choose dietary preferences (vegan, keto, gluten-free, etc.)
  • Enter your zip code to find nearby stores
2
List Building

Build or generate your shopping list

Add items manually, scan barcodes, or let the AI generate a complete week's worth of groceries based on your meal plan. Every item is linked to live price data across your local stores.

  • Manual entry or barcode scan
  • AI auto-generates a list from your meal plan
  • Reuse previous lists with one tap
3
Price Intelligence

Compare prices across 40+ stores

$mart Cart scans prices at every major grocery store near you — updated multiple times daily. See your full cart total at each store instantly, and identify exactly which items to buy where for maximum savings.

  • Real-time prices updated throughout the day
  • Side-by-side store comparison for every item
  • Automatic coupon and sale matching
4
In-Store

Shop and track in real time

Check off items as you shop. Your running total and macro intake update with each check-off so you always know where you stand — on budget and on your nutrition goals — before you hit the checkout.

  • Live running cart total and savings counter
  • Macros and calories update as you shop
  • Substitution suggestions when items are out of stock
AI Features

Powered by artificial intelligence

$mart Cart's AI layer does the heavy lifting — surfacing the right deals, building your meal plan, and learning your preferences over time.

01

Smart Price Engine

Our AI continuously monitors prices across all supported stores, detects price drops, and automatically surfaces the best deal for every item on your list.

02

Personalized Meal Planner

Tell the AI your goals, restrictions, and budget — it builds a complete weekly meal plan and generates an optimized grocery list to match.

03

Nutrition Intelligence

The macro calculator learns which foods help you hit your targets and proactively suggests swaps that improve nutrition without increasing cost.
